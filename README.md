# Lucas Weinstein Porfolio
A personal website to showcase who I am, what I’ve built, and what I’m passionate about.
It includes my projects, skills, and background as a computer science student and developer.

Live Here: https://lucas-weinstein-portfolio.vercel.app

## About

This portfolio serves as a central hub for:
- Highlighting my projects and technical experience
- Providing an overview of my skills and interests
- Offering easy ways to connect and reach out
It’s designed to be simple, responsive, and easy to update as I grow.

## Sections

- Home — A brief introduction about who I am
- About Me — Education and personal background
- Projects — A showcase of key work and portfolio items
- Skills — Languages, tools, and frameworks I’m proficient in
- Contact — Ways to get in touch (LinkedIn, Email, etc.)
